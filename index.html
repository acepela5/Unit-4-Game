Object of the Game

Try to match your number with the computer's

generated number

How to Play

Click on the characters to see what number they hide.

Win the game by matching numerb's with the computer.

Loose the game by going over the computer's number.

At the end of each game, the computer will generate a

new number and the pictures will be given new values.
